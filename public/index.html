A Platform for Providers

Enhance Patient Care Efficiency with CSH Rounds: Your All-in-One Provider-Centric App

Our application allows specialists such as neurologists to consolidate the patients they see across multiple facilities in one easy-to-use mobile app, to create admissions or discharge a patient, and also map CPT codes to the ICD codes pertaining to the patient.
